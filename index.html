2020 Weird Gloop Year in Review

2020 has been a humanitarian disaster on a global scale and a fantastic year of growth for the RuneScapes Wiki. We've forged new collaborations and built new tools to make us the best possible resource for RuneScape, Old School RuneScape, and RuneScape Classic. We are Weird Gloop, the organisation behind the site, and we'd like to share this year's successes with you.
